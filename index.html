Avengers Series

Avengers:1
The Avengers, They were initially brought together by S.H.I.E.L.D. to combat Loki, Thor's adoptive brother. The team was initially formed by Nick Fury to "fight the battles that S.H.I.E.L.D. never could".
Avengers:2
In Avengers: Age of Ultron, They were disbanded due to the actions of Tony Stark; who betrayed his former team to enforce the Sokovia Accords, but a small group consisting of Captain America, Black Widow, and Falcon continued to operate in the shadows.




Avengers:3
In Avengers: Infinity War, The team were reassembled by Steve, but many fell to the Snap caused by Thanos wielding all six Infinity Stones by means of a powerful gauntlet.
Avengers:4
In Avengers: Endgame, the Avengers with the help from Scott Lang was able to use the Pym Particles to travel into the past to retrieve the Infinity Stones and reverse the Decimation. The Avengers, along side the restored fallen Avengers, Masters of the Mystic Arts, the Guardians of the Galaxy, the armies of Wakanda, New Asgard and the Ravagers was finally able to defeat Thanos and his forces. However, the mission left both Tony Stark and Natalia Romanoff dead. Vision also remained dead as he was killed by Thanos before the Decimation.
